### 검사도구  METRECC(MEasuring TeacheR Enacted Computing Curriculum)

* 목적 : 전세계 교실의 pedagogy, practice, resources, experiences 이해

* curriculum 2개로 나눔 
	1. Intended(ex. CSTA) : Country Report
